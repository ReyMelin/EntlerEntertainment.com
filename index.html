About

Entler Entertainment is the creative vision of Thomas Richard Entler a storyteller, innovator, and creator of interactive experiences. With two published books, a groundbreaking augmented reality mobile game, and a new social media platform in development, Entler Entertainment explores the space where imagination meets technology. From words on a page to worlds in your pocket, the mission is to create experiences that entertain, inspire, and connect people in meaningful ways.
